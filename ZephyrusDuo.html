Zephyrus Duo 16

The Zephyrus Duo 16 is a very capable multi-purpose laptop and a computer more powerful and able to run cooler than the standard ROG models of this generation. However, the two displays are its most important selling point, and the main reason you're going to pay a hefty premium for this Duo 16 over standard designs with similar specs and capabilities. So make sure you want and need those two screens on your laptop.
